# Strategy: Path to "Obvious Choice" — July 2026

Research-backed strategy report (5 research streams over the hobby's own
venues + comparable-platform growth playbooks). Full citations in the
session transcript; confidence flags preserved.

## The one-sentence diagnosis

MHH has more product than every incumbent combined — the binding
constraint is **discoverability and trust**: ~1 indexed Google page, no
social presence, no app-store listing, anonymous About page, in a hobby
that has been burned twice by infrastructure collapse and lives inside
Facebook groups.

## Verified landscape

- FB B/S/T groups dominate commerce (7+ groups; sizes unverifiable).
  Blab restored Jan 2025, ~7k members. Discord minor. Active TikTok
  maker scene. BreyerFest ≈35k attendees.
- Pain (HIGH confidence): venue fragmentation + duplicate-listing risk;
  infrastructure fragility (Blab dark ~2 yrs; MH$P ransomwared →
  Model Horse Connection); NAMHSA reports ~1/3 of NAN cards submitted
  are invalid/expired/misfilled; scam anxiety encoded in group rules;
  PayPal F&F pressure. NOBODY in the hobby has ever offered escrow.
  Provenance-loss pain = plausible but UNVERIFIED (validate w/ users).
- Competitors are single-purpose and volunteer-run: OMHPS (photo shows),
  MYMHDB (inventory), breyervalueguide (paid values), IDYB (static ID),
  Model Horse Connection (classifieds), ModelHorses.com (legacy registry
  — watch it). Nobody serves live showholders at all. NAMHSA has no
  tech partner and public cost pressure on NAN 2026.
- Playbooks: Discogs/TCGplayer/Reverb = free SEO-public database +
  price data BEFORE marketplace; PSA/MyFigureCollection/Ravelry =
  registry tracking as retention/lock-in; Ravelry/Whatnot = court power
  users (give income/status); StockX/TCGplayer = trust infrastructure
  beats eBay; free-to-list, fee-on-sale; Ravelry migration driver =
  structured-data UTILITY Facebook can't copy.

## The 8 moves (ranked)

1. **Open the reference database + Blue Book to the public web.**
   10,500+ releases → 10,000+ indexed pages w/ price teasers. The
   Discogs wedge. "What's my Breyer worth" has no good answer today.
2. **Court showholders; free live-show hosting; public results pages.**
   Uncontested vacuum (no live-show software exists anywhere). Each
   show delivers its entrants. "Claim your horse" from results.
   White-glove onboard 3–5 showholders.
3. **Digital NAN-card companion + public verify links → then pitch
   NAMHSA.** Their own docs admit the paper system fails 1/3 of the
   time. Companion works unilaterally; partnership is upside.
4. **Fix the trust story:** named founder on About, full CSV/PDF data
   export, plain-English continuity/backup statement. Cheap; directly
   answers the "will this die like Blab/MH$P" objection.
5. **Meet Facebook where it lives:** rich OG share pages for every
   horse/show/card (drop-in-a-thread quality), Hoofprint-verified
   badges usable in FB listings, migration concierge for 2–3 small
   clubs. Don't fight the megagroups yet.
6. **Portable reputation:** public per-seller reference pages seeded
   from Safe-Trade completions; escrow converts the high-anxiety
   resin/pre-sale transactions.
7. **Table stakes:** Play Store TWA wrapper; official FB page +
   Instagram; visible liveness (weekly virtual shows, activity feed).
   BreyerFest week = the year's best acquisition moment.
8. **Estate/insurance reports as the Pro monetization spine** (converts,
   doesn't acquire). Validate provenance-pain messaging with users first.

## Sequence

- **30 days:** public+indexed release/Blue Book pages (sitemap, OG);
  de-anonymize About; data export + continuity statement; BreyerFest
  push; recruit 3–5 showholders; official FB/IG; start Play Store TWA.
- **30–90 days:** public show results + claim-your-horse; NAN-card
  companion + verify links; seller reference pages; rich share pages;
  migrate 2–3 small clubs; send NAMHSA pitch w/ working demo; weekly
  virtual show cadence.
- **1 year:** 30–50 shows hosted; Blue Book cited in FB threads;
  app stores; marketplace liquidity push behind Safe-Trade; NAMHSA
  pilot or de-facto-standard companion; consider read API/embeds.

**Audience order: showholders → high-value sellers (resins) → casual
collectors.**

## Risks / unverified

Provenance-pain unvalidated; FB group sizes unknown; ModelHorses.com
overlap unquantified; NAMHSA slow — never block on them; hobby size
has no published estimate (proxies suggest tens of thousands — a
Ravelry-scale niche win, right-sized for a 2-person freemium shop).
